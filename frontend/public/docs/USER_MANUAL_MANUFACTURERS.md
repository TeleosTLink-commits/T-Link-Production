## 5. CoA Lookup

### 5.1 What is a CoA?

A **Certificate of Analysis (CoA)** is an official document that certifies the quality, purity, and composition of a chemical sample. It includes:

- Lot/batch number
- Test results
- Certification date
- Expiration date (if applicable)

### 5.2 Finding a CoA

1. From the Dashboard, click **CoA Lookup**
2. You'll see a dropdown list of all samples
3. Select the sample you need
4. Click **Get Certificate**

### 5.3 Viewing Results

If a CoA is available, you'll see:

- Sample name
- Lot number
- Certification date
- Expiration date (if applicable)
- **Download** button

### 5.4 Downloading the CoA

1. Click **Download**
2. The CoA PDF will either:
    - Open in a new browser tab, OR
    - Download directly to your computer
3. You can print or save the PDF for your records

### 5.5 No CoA Found

If no CoA is available for your selected sample:

- Contact support to request one
- The CoA may be pending upload
- Verify you selected the correct lot number

## 7. Requesting a Shipment

### 7.1 Starting a Request

1. From the Dashboard, click **Request Shipment**
2. The shipment request form will open

### 7.2 Contact Information

Fill in your contact details:

| Field | Description | Required |
| ----- | ----------- | -------- |
| First Name | Your first name | Yes |
| Last Name | Your last name | Yes |
| Company | Your company name | Optional |
| Phone | Contact phone number | Yes |

### 7.3 Delivery Address

Enter the destination address for the shipment:

| Field | Description | Required |
| ----- | ----------- | -------- |
| Street Address | Street address, suite/unit number | Yes |
| City | Destination city | Yes |
| State/Province | State or province code | Yes |
| ZIP/Postal Code | Postal code | Yes |
| Country | Default: US | Yes |

> **Important:** Ensure the address is accurate. FedEx will validate the address during processing, and incorrect addresses may cause delays.

### 7.4 Emergency Contact

For safety compliance, an **Emergency Contact Phone** is required for all shipments. This is the number that can be reached 24/7 in case of a shipping emergency (especially for hazardous materials).

### 7.5 Selecting Samples

**Adding Your First Sample:**

1. Use the dropdown to select a sample (shows chemical name and lot number)
2. Enter the **Quantity Requested** (in the appropriate units)
3. Select the **Unit** (ml, g, kg, etc.)

**Adding Multiple Samples:**

- Click **+ Add Another Sample** to add more samples to the same shipment
- You can add up to **10 samples** per shipment request
- Click the **Remove** button next to any sample to delete it

### 7.6 HazMat Information

When you select a sample, the system automatically displays any hazardous materials information:

- **UN Number** - United Nations hazard ID
- **Hazard Class** - DOT classification (1-9)
- **Packing Group** - Severity level (I, II, or III)
- **Proper Shipping Name** - Official DOT name

> **Note:** This information is read-only and comes from the lab's sample database. If the information appears incorrect, contact lab support.

### 7.7 Scheduling and Instructions

| Field | Description |
| ----- | ----------- |
| Scheduled Ship Date | Preferred shipping date (optional) |
| Special Instructions | Any special handling notes |
| International Shipment | Check if shipping outside the US |

### 7.8 Reviewing Your Request

Before submitting, review all information carefully:

- Verify contact information is correct
- Confirm delivery address is complete
- Check sample quantities are accurate
- Ensure emergency contact is provided

### 7.9 Submitting the Request

1. Click **Submit Request**
2. If successful, you'll see a confirmation page with:
    - Request confirmation number
    - Summary of your order
    - Next steps information
3. You'll receive a **confirmation email** at your registered email address

### 7.10 What Happens Next?

After you submit a request:

1. **Initiated** - Your request is received and queued for processing
2. **Processing** - Lab staff are preparing your shipment
3. **Shipped** - Your order is on the way with FedEx
4. **Delivered** - Package has been delivered

You can track the status at any time in **My Shipments**.

## 8. Tracking Your Shipments

### 8.1 Accessing My Shipments

From the Dashboard, click **My Shipments**.

### 8.2 Shipment Overview

At the top of the page, you'll see summary cards showing:

- **All Requests** - Total number of shipments
- **Initiated** - New requests pending processing
- **Processing** - Shipments being prepared
- **Shipped** - Orders in transit
- **Delivered** - Completed shipments

Click any card to filter the list by that status.

### 8.3 Filtering Shipments

Use the **Filter by Status** dropdown to show:

- All Shipments
- Initiated only
- Processing only
- Shipped only
- Delivered only

### 8.4 Shipment Details

Each shipment card shows:

- **Lot Number** - The sample's lot identifier
- **Quantity** - Amount shipped
- **Status** - Current status with color indicator
- **Created Date** - When you submitted the request
- **Scheduled Ship Date** - When shipment is planned
- **Tracking Number** - FedEx tracking (when available)

### 8.5 Status Colors

| Color | Status | Meaning |
| ----- | ------ | ------- |

## 10. FAQ & Troubleshooting

### Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: How do I register for an account?**  
A: Your email must first be authorized by Ajwa Labs. Contact your representative, then click "Create Account" on the login page.

**Q: I forgot my password. What do I do?**  
A: Contact support at <jhunzie@ajwalabs.com> to request a password reset.

**Q: Why can't I see my past orders?**  
A: The portal shows orders linked to your account. If you recently registered, previous orders may be under a different account. Contact support.

**Q: How long does shipping take?**  
A: Shipping times depend on the service level:

- Ground: 3-7 business days
- Express Saver: 2-3 business days
- Priority Overnight: Next business day

**Q: Can I cancel or modify an order?**  
A: Contact lab support immediately. Orders may be modified if they haven't started processing yet.

**Q: What if my package arrives damaged?**  
A: Contact lab support immediately with photos of the damage. Keep all packaging materials for the carrier's inspection.

**Q: How do I update my contact information?**  
A: Contact support to update your account details.

### Troubleshooting Common Issues

#### Problem: I can't log in

**Solutions:**

1. Verify your email is spelled correctly
2. Check that CAPS LOCK is off
3. If you've failed 5 times, wait 15 minutes and try again
4. Contact support for a password reset

#### Problem: The page won't load

**Solutions:**

1. Refresh the page (Ctrl+F5 or Cmd+Shift+R)
2. Clear your browser cache
3. Try a different web browser
4. Check your internet connection

#### Problem: I can't download a CoA

**Solutions:**

1. Check if your browser is blocking popups
2. Try right-clicking the download button â†’ "Open in new tab"
3. Try a different browser
4. Contact support if the CoA isn't available

#### Problem: My shipment is stuck in "Processing"

**Solutions:**

1. Processing typically takes 1-2 business days
2. Check for any emails from Ajwa Labs regarding issues
3. Contact lab support if processing exceeds 3 days

#### Problem: Tracking number isn't working

**Solutions:**

1. Wait 1-2 hours - tracking info may not be immediately available
2. Verify the number is complete (no spaces or extra characters)
3. Go directly to fedex.com and enter the number manually
