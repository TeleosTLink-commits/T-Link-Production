# T-Link Platform Training Script
## Complete User Training Guide for Voice-Over

---

## INTRODUCTION (0:00-0:30)

Welcome to T-Link, the comprehensive laboratory sample management and logistics platform developed by AAL Digital Development. This training will guide you through accessing, creating an account, and navigating the T-Link platform. 

T-Link serves two types of users: Manufacturers who submit samples for testing, and Internal staff who process and manage those samples. We'll cover both user experiences in this training.

---

## PART 1: MANUFACTURER USER GUIDE (External Users)

### Section 1.1: Accessing the Platform (0:30-1:00)

To access T-Link as a manufacturer, open your web browser and navigate to the T-Link platform URL provided by your account administrator. You'll arrive at the T-Link landing page featuring the platform logo and two main options: Login and Register.

If you're a new user, you'll need to create an account. If you already have an account, you can proceed directly to login.

### Section 1.2: Creating a Manufacturer Account (1:00-3:00)

**Step 1: Starting Registration**
- Click the "Register" button on the landing page
- You'll be taken to the manufacturer registration form

**Step 2: Entering Company Information**
The registration form has several sections. Let's go through each one:

First, enter your **Company Information**:
- Company Name: Enter your organization's full legal name
- Company Address: Provide your complete business address
- City: Enter your city
- State: Select your state from the dropdown menu
- ZIP Code: Enter your 5-digit ZIP code
- Country: The default is United States, but you can change if needed

**Step 3: Primary Contact Information**
Next, provide your **Primary Contact Details**:
- First Name: Your first name
- Last Name: Your last name  
- Email Address: Use a valid business email - this will be your username
- Phone Number: Enter your direct phone number with area code
- Job Title: Your position within the company

**Step 4: Account Security**
Now set up your **Account Credentials**:
- Username: This is automatically set to your email address
- Password: Create a strong password with at least 8 characters
- Confirm Password: Re-enter your password to verify

**Step 5: Manufacturer Verification**
Provide your **Certification Information**:
- Manufacturer License Number: Enter your valid manufacturer license ID
- Certification Type: Select your certification (such as GMP, ISO, or other)
- Upload Certification Document: Click "Choose File" and upload a PDF or image of your certification

**Step 6: Terms and Submission**
- Check the box to agree to the Terms and Conditions
- Review all information for accuracy
- Click "Register" to submit your application

**Step 7: Approval Process**
After submission, you'll see a message indicating your application is pending approval. Internal staff will review your application and verify your credentials. You'll receive an email notification once your account is approved, typically within 1-2 business days.

### Section 1.3: Manufacturer Login Process (3:00-3:45)

**Once your account is approved:**
- Return to the T-Link landing page
- Click "Login"
- Enter your email address (username)
- Enter your password
- Click "Sign In"

If you forget your password, click the "Forgot Password?" link to reset it via email.

Upon successful login, you'll be directed to your Manufacturer Dashboard.

### Section 1.4: Manufacturer Dashboard Overview (3:45-4:30)

The Manufacturer Dashboard is your home base. Let's explore the layout:

**Header Section (Top of page):**
- Left side: T-Link logo and "Manufacturer Portal" heading
- Right side: 
  - "Contact Support" button - for technical or lab assistance
  - "Sign Out" button - to log out of your account

**Welcome Banner:**
Below the header, you'll see a personalized welcome message with your name and the current date.

**Quick Statistics Cards:**
Four cards display your key metrics:
- Total Samples: Count of all samples you've submitted
- Active Shipments: Number of shipments currently in transit
- Pending Results: Samples awaiting test results
- Completed Tests: Samples with finalized test results

**Navigation Menu:**
The main navigation shows five key sections with icons:
1. **Submit Sample** - Submit new samples for testing
2. **My Samples** - View and track all your submitted samples
3. **Shipment Request** - Request shipment logistics for multiple samples
4. **My Shipments** - Track your shipment requests
5. **Support** - Contact technical support or lab staff

**Footer:**
At the bottom, you'll see "Developed and operated by" with the AAL Digital Development logo.

### Section 1.5: Submitting a Sample (4:30-7:00)

**Step 1: Accessing the Form**
- Click "Submit Sample" from the dashboard menu
- You'll see the Sample Submission Form with a green gradient header

**Step 2: Sample Information**
Fill out the **Sample Information** section:
- Sample ID: Enter your internal sample identification number
- Lot Number: Provide the production lot number
- Product Name: Enter the product name
- Product Type: Select from dropdown (e.g., Cannabis Product, Extract, etc.)
- Sample Description: Provide detailed description of the sample

**Step 3: Test Requirements**
In the **Test Requirements** section:
- Test Method: Select the required test from the dropdown (e.g., Potency Analysis, Pesticide Screening, Heavy Metals, Microbial Testing, Terpene Profile, Residual Solvents)
- Additional Notes: Add any special instructions or requirements
- Priority Level: Select Standard or Rush processing
- Expected Results Date: Choose when you need results by

**Step 4: Shipping Information**
Complete the **Shipping Information** section:
- Shipping Method: Select your preferred carrier (FedEx, UPS, USPS, or Hand Delivery)
- Tracking Number: Enter the tracking number if already shipped
- Expected Delivery Date: Select the anticipated delivery date
- Special Handling: Note any special requirements (e.g., "Keep Refrigerated", "Fragile", "Light Sensitive")

**Step 5: Documentation**
Upload supporting documents:
- Click "Choose File" to select your Certificate of Analysis (CoA) or other relevant documents
- Supported formats: PDF, JPG, PNG
- You can upload multiple files if needed

**Step 6: Review and Submit**
- Review all information carefully
- Make any necessary corrections
- Click "Submit Sample" at the bottom
- You'll see a success message with your sample submission ID
- Click "View My Samples" to see your submission or "Submit Another Sample" to continue

### Section 1.6: Viewing and Managing Your Samples (7:00-8:30)

**Accessing My Samples:**
- Click "My Samples" from the dashboard menu
- You'll see a searchable table of all your submitted samples

**Understanding the Sample List:**
The table displays:
- Sample ID: Your reference number
- Lot Number: Production lot
- Product Name: Product identification  
- Test Method: Type of testing requested
- Status: Current stage (Submitted, In Testing, Completed, On Hold)
- Submitted Date: When you submitted the sample
- Actions: View details, Download CoA (when available)

**Status Indicators:**
- **Blue "Submitted"**: Sample received, awaiting processing
- **Orange "In Testing"**: Sample is currently being analyzed
- **Green "Completed"**: Testing finished, results available
- **Red "On Hold"**: Issue requires attention

**Sample Details:**
- Click the "View" button to see complete sample information
- View test results and timeline
- Download Certificate of Analysis (CoA) when ready

**Searching and Filtering:**
- Use the search bar to find specific samples by ID, lot number, or product name
- Filter by status using the dropdown menu
- Sort columns by clicking column headers

### Section 1.7: Requesting Shipments (8:30-11:00)

When you need to ship multiple samples together, use the Shipment Request feature.

**Step 1: Start a Shipment Request**
- Click "Shipment Request" from the dashboard menu
- You'll see the Multi-Sample Shipment Request form

**Step 2: Add Samples to Your Shipment**
For each sample you want to include:
- Click "Add Sample" to create a new sample entry card
- Fill in each sample's information:
  - Sample ID
  - Lot Number  
  - Product Name
  - Test Method (select from dropdown)
  - Sample Quantity (number and unit)
  - Special Handling instructions if needed
- Click "Add Another Sample" to include additional samples in this shipment
- You can remove a sample by clicking the remove button on its card

**Step 3: Shipment Details**
Complete the **Shipment Information** section:
- Shipping Carrier: Select FedEx, UPS, USPS, or Hand Delivery
- Service Level: Choose Ground, Express, Overnight, etc.
- Preferred Pickup Date: Select when you want the shipment picked up
- Preferred Pickup Time: Choose your time window

**Step 4: Pickup Location**
Enter your **Pickup Address**:
- Address Line 1 and 2
- City, State, ZIP Code
- Contact Name for pickup
- Contact Phone Number

**Step 5: Delivery Information**
The delivery address is pre-filled with the lab address, but verify:
- Lab receiving address
- Lab contact information

**Step 6: Packaging Information**
Specify your **Package Details**:
- Total Weight (with unit: lbs or kg)
- Package Dimensions (Length x Width x Height in inches)
- Package Type: Select Box, Envelope, Tube, or Custom
- Number of Packages if multiple boxes

**Step 7: Special Instructions**
- Check boxes for special handling:
  - Hazardous Material (if applicable)
  - Temperature Controlled
  - Fragile
  - Signature Required
- Add any additional shipping notes in the text area

**Step 8: Review and Submit**
- Review the summary showing all samples and shipping details
- Make any corrections needed
- Click "Submit Shipment Request"
- You'll receive a confirmation with your shipment request ID
- An email confirmation will be sent to your registered email

### Section 1.8: Tracking Your Shipments (11:00-12:30)

**Accessing My Shipments:**
- Click "My Shipments" from the dashboard menu
- You'll see your shipment requests dashboard

**Dashboard Overview:**
The top section shows summary cards:
- Total Shipments: All shipment requests you've made
- Active: Currently in transit or processing
- Delivered: Successfully completed shipments  
- Pending: Awaiting pickup or processing

**Shipment List:**
Each shipment card displays:
- Shipment Request ID
- Number of samples included
- Carrier and service level
- Current status with color-coded badge
- Requested pickup date
- Tracking number (when assigned)
- Timeline showing shipment progress

**Shipment Statuses:**
- **Pending**: Request received, awaiting processing
- **Confirmed**: Pickup scheduled and confirmed
- **In Transit**: Package picked up and moving
- **Delivered**: Successfully received at lab
- **Cancelled**: Request cancelled

**Viewing Shipment Details:**
- Click on a shipment card to expand and see:
  - Complete list of all samples in the shipment
  - Full shipping and pickup information
  - Real-time tracking updates
  - Timeline of all status changes
  - Contact information for support

**Filtering and Searching:**
- Use the status filter dropdown to view specific shipment types
- Search by shipment ID or tracking number
- View date ranges for historical shipments

### Section 1.9: Contacting Support (12:30-13:30)

When you need assistance, T-Link provides dedicated support channels.

**Accessing Support:**
- Click "Support" from the dashboard menu, or
- Click "Contact Support" button in the header

**Support Form:**
You'll see two support options:

**Technical Support:**
- For platform issues, login problems, or technical difficulties
- Fill out the form:
  - Subject: Brief description of the issue
  - Message: Detailed explanation of your problem
  - Your email is pre-filled from your account
  - Your name is pre-filled from your account
- Messages are sent directly to the technical support team at jhunzie@ajwalabs.com
- Click "Submit Tech Support Request"

**Lab Support:**
- For sample questions, test inquiries, or result clarifications
- Fill out the form:
  - Subject: Brief description of your inquiry
  - Message: Detailed question or concern about samples/testing
  - Your contact information is pre-filled
- Messages are sent directly to the lab team at eboak@ajwalabs.com
- Click "Submit Lab Support Request"

**After Submission:**
- You'll receive a confirmation message
- A confirmation email is sent to you
- The appropriate team receives your request
- Expect a response within 1 business day for standard inquiries
- Urgent issues are typically addressed within 4 hours

## PART 2: INTERNAL USER GUIDE (Staff Users)

### Section 2.1: Accessing the Platform (13:30-14:00)

Internal staff access T-Link through the same URL, but your login credentials and permissions are different. You'll use the same landing page with Login and Register buttons.

However, internal staff accounts are not self-registered. Your account must be created by a Super Administrator.

### Section 2.2: Internal Account Creation (14:00-15:00)

**Note: This section is for Super Administrators creating staff accounts**

Internal user accounts are created through the Super Admin Panel:

**Step 1: Access Admin Panel**
- Super Admin logs into their account
- Clicks "Super Admin Panel" from the dashboard

**Step 2: Navigate to User Management**
- Click "User Management" tab
- Click "Add New User" button

**Step 3: Enter User Information**
- First Name and Last Name
- Email Address (becomes their username)
- Phone Number
- Job Title/Department
- Temporary Password (user will change on first login)

**Step 4: Set User Role**
The role determines what the user can access:
- **Lab Technician**: Access to test methods, sample inventory, view-only
- **Lab Manager**: Full access to test methods, sample inventory, can edit and approve
- **Logistics Coordinator**: Access to shipment management, sample receiving
- **Admin**: Access to all areas except user management
- **Super Admin**: Full platform access including user management

**Step 5: Set Permissions**
- Enable specific feature access
- Set department assignment
- Configure notification preferences

**Step 6: Create Account**
- Click "Create User"
- System generates the account
- Automated email with login credentials sent to the new user

### Section 2.3: Internal User Login (15:00-15:30)

**First-Time Login:**
- Navigate to the T-Link platform
- Click "Login"
- Enter your email address (provided by admin)
- Enter your temporary password (from welcome email)
- Click "Sign In"
- You'll be prompted to change your password immediately
- Create a new strong password
- Confirm the new password
- Click "Update Password"

**Subsequent Logins:**
- Navigate to platform
- Click "Login"
- Enter email and your new password

### Section 2.6: Sample Inventory Management (18:00-20:30)

**Accessing Sample Inventory:**
- Click "Sample Inventory" from the dashboard
- You'll see the comprehensive Sample Inventory Management system

**Dashboard Overview:**
Summary cards at the top show:
- Total Samples in System
- Samples In Testing (currently being analyzed)
- Pending Results (awaiting finalization)
- Completed Today (finished within last 24 hours)

**Sample Inventory Table:**
Displays all samples with columns:
- Sample ID
- Manufacturer/Client Name
- Lot Number
- Product Name
- Test Method
- Status (color-coded badge)
- Priority (Standard or Rush)
- Received Date
- Expected Completion Date
- Assigned Technician
- Actions (View, Edit, Update Status, Print Labels)

**Status Workflow:**
Samples progress through these statuses:
1. **Submitted** (Blue) - Sample request received from manufacturer
2. **Received** (Teal) - Physical sample arrived at lab
3. **In Testing** (Orange) - Currently being analyzed
4. **Results Review** (Purple) - Testing complete, awaiting QC review
5. **Completed** (Green) - All testing finished, CoA generated
6. **On Hold** (Red) - Issue requiring attention
7. **Rejected** (Dark Red) - Sample failed acceptance criteria

**Receiving a Sample:**
When a sample arrives at the lab:
- Locate the sample in the inventory (search by Sample ID or manufacturer)
- Click "Update Status"
- Change status from "Submitted" to "Received"
- Enter additional information:
  - Physical sample condition (Good, Damaged, Questionable)
  - Actual quantity received
  - Temperature upon receipt (if applicable)
  - Sample appearance notes
  - Assign to lab technician
  - Assign to storage location (freezer, refrigerator, room temperature)
  - Upload photo of sample if needed
- Click "Save Updates"

**Processing a Sample:**
As testing progresses:
- Technician opens the sample record
- Updates status to "In Testing"
- Records testing start time
- Enters preliminary data as testing proceeds
- Uploads raw data files
- Enters final results when complete
- Changes status to "Results Review"

**Reviewing Results:**
Lab Manager or QC staff:
- Opens samples in "Results Review" status
- Verifies all test data
- Checks calculations
- Reviews against acceptance criteria
- Checks for any anomalies
- Approves or requests re-test if needed
- If approved, changes status to "Completed"

**Generating Certificate of Analysis (CoA):**
Once results are approved:
- Click "Generate CoA" button
- System automatically populates:
  - Sample information
  - Test results
  - Methodology references
  - QC data
  - Technician and reviewer signatures
  - Date of testing and report
- Review PDF preview
- Make any formatting adjustments if needed
- Click "Finalize CoA"
- CoA is automatically:
  - Saved to sample record
  - Sent to manufacturer via email
  - Available for download in manufacturer's account

**Searching and Filtering:**
- Quick search bar for Sample ID, Lot Number, or Product Name
- Advanced filters:
  - Manufacturer/Client
  - Test Method
  - Status
  - Priority Level
  - Date range (received, expected completion)
  - Assigned technician
  - Storage location
- Export filtered results to Excel for reporting

**Viewing Sample History:**
Click "View" on any sample to see complete timeline:
- Submission details
- Receipt details and condition
- All status changes with timestamps
- Testing data entries
- Result reviews and approvals
- Communications with manufacturer
- Document uploads (CoAs, images, raw data)
- Audit trail of all changes

### Section 2.7: Shipment Logistics Management (20:30-22:30)

**Accessing Shipment Logistics:**
- Click "Shipment Logistics" from the dashboard
- You'll see the Shipment Management interface

**Dashboard Summary:**
Top section displays key metrics:
- Pending Pickups: Shipments awaiting pickup scheduling
- In Transit: Packages currently moving
- Delivered Today: Shipments received today
- Processing: Shipments being unpacked and checked in

**Shipment Request Queue:**
Table shows all manufacturer shipment requests:
- Request ID
- Manufacturer Name
- Number of Samples
- Requested Pickup Date
- Carrier and Service Level
- Status (New, Scheduled, Picked Up, In Transit, Delivered, Completed)
- Assigned Logistics Coordinator
- Actions (View, Process, Update)

**Processing New Shipment Requests:**

**Step 1: Review Request**
- Click "View" on a new shipment request
- Review all details:
  - List of all samples included
  - Pickup address and contact info
  - Requested date and time
  - Special handling requirements
  - Package information

**Step 2: Validate Information**
- Verify pickup address is accurate
- Check for any special requirements
- Confirm all sample information is complete
- Note any hazardous materials declarations

**Step 3: Schedule Pickup**
- Click "Schedule Pickup" button
- Enter carrier details:
  - Confirm carrier (FedEx, UPS, USPS)
  - Enter account number for shipping
  - Select actual service level
  - Set pickup date (confirm or modify requested date)
  - Set pickup time window
- Generate shipping label:
  - System auto-fills from/to addresses
  - Enters package details (weight, dimensions)
  - Creates label PDF
  - Assigns tracking number

**Step 4: Confirm with Manufacturer**
- System automatically sends email to manufacturer with:
  - Pickup confirmation
  - Scheduled date and time
  - Tracking number
  - Shipping label (PDF attachment)
  - Preparation instructions
- Update request status to "Scheduled"

**Step 5: Track Shipment**
- As shipment progresses, update status:
  - "Picked Up" when carrier collects package
  - "In Transit" as it moves
  - "Out for Delivery" on delivery day
- System automatically pulls tracking updates from carrier APIs
- Notifications sent to manufacturer at each stage

**Receiving Shipments at Lab:**

**Step 1: Delivery Notification**
- When package arrives, locate the shipment in system
- Click "Mark as Delivered"
- Enter delivery information:
  - Actual delivery date and time
  - Received by (staff member name)
  - Package condition (Good, Damaged, Other)
  - Temperature if applicable

**Step 2: Unpack and Verify**
- Click "Begin Inspection"
- Check each sample against packing list:
  - Verify Sample IDs match
  - Check sample condition
  - Confirm quantities
  - Note any discrepancies
- For each sample:
  - Scan or enter Sample ID
  - Mark as "Received" in inventory
  - Assign storage location
  - Take photos if needed

**Step 3: Complete Check-In**
- Review the checklist:
  - All samples accounted for ✓
  - All samples in good condition ✓
  - Documentation complete ✓
- Click "Complete Shipment"
- System updates:
  - Shipment status to "Completed"
  - All associated samples to "Received"
  - Manufacturer notified via email

**Step 4: Handle Discrepancies**
If there are issues:
- Select "Report Issue"
- Choose issue type:
  - Damaged package
  - Missing samples
  - Damaged samples
  - Incorrect samples
  - Documentation missing
- Add photos and description
- System alerts manufacturer and lab management
- Opens support ticket for resolution

**Searching and Reporting:**
- Search shipments by ID, tracking number, or manufacturer
- Filter by status, date range, carrier
- Generate reports:
  - Daily receiving report
  - Monthly shipment volume
  - Carrier performance metrics
  - Turnaround time analysis
- Export data to Excel for further analysis
